About Us

Why Memory Mitra?

Understanding the mission behind early dementia detection in rural communities.

🧠

Dementia Awareness Matters

Over 8.8 million people in India are estimated to have dementia, yet most cases go undiagnosed due to lack of awareness and access to clinical tools. Early detection can significantly improve quality of life and reduce caregiver burden. Community-level screening bridges this critical gap.

👩‍🦳

Focus on Elderly Women

Elderly women in rural areas are disproportionately affected by dementia, yet least likely to receive timely diagnosis. Social isolation, limited healthcare access, and cultural stigma mean symptoms go unnoticed for years. Memory Mitra specifically reaches this underserved group through trusted community spaces.

🎉

Festivals as Touchpoints

Festivals and community gatherings provide a natural, non-clinical environment where elderly women participate actively. Using culturally familiar activities — like identifying ingredients, recognizing devotional songs, and recalling festival traditions — reduces anxiety and makes screening feel comfortable and dignified.

Our Approach

Simple, Dignified, Culturally Rooted

Memory Mitra combines the familiarity of traditional festival culture with simple cognitive screening tools developed for non-clinical settings — no doctors required, no clinical environment, just community volunteers and culturally meaningful tasks.

✅ No medical background required for screening
✅ Culturally familiar questions reduce test anxiety
✅ Participants receive immediate guidance and referrals
